PLATFORM

Mayor Peyton Jamison has a proven track record of results - and a plan to keep Milton thriving!

Preserving Milton’s Character

  • Committed to maintaining Milton’s charm and rural character while planning for a thoughtful future

  • 12 years of zoning experience, always making sustainment of Milton’s unique identity the top priority

  • Has never – and will never – support sewer expansion

Managing your tax dollars effectively

  • Kept his promise to cut taxes and lowered the city’s millage rate 7% since taking office.

  • Committed to reducing the millage rate even further during the next four years

  • City was ranked #1 nationally in customer service by Milton residents – even after cutting taxes!

Prioritizing Public Safety

  • Ranked the #3 safest city in the state of Georgia

  • One of the only cities in the region with fully staffed police and fire departments

  • Bolstered school safety and added further protections for students and teachers

Championing Parks and Green Space

  • Driving force behind Milton’s long-term Parks and Recreation Master Plan

  • Moved the City forward with plans for a new active sports complex in the Deerfield area

  • Delivered permanent protection for Birmingham Park
